## 도입부 (전사본 발췌) — ⚠️ 영어 자막 기반

The theme for our program this month is “Treasure Jehovah’s Reminders.” And it’s based on Psalm 19:7, where we’re told: “The reminder of Jehovah is trustworthy, making the inexperienced one wise.” In the original language, the word here translated as “reminder” comes from a root word meaning “to go over again, to repeat.” Reminders have always been an essential part of Jehovah’s teaching. In ancient Israel, God’s people continually received commandments, regulations, and reminders. In fact, Jehovah tells us that he sent his prophets “again and again” to repeatedly remind his people of their responsibilities.
