## 도입부 (전사본 발췌) — ⚠️ 영어 자막 기반

My theme for this month’s broadcast is “Young Ones —Build Your Future With Jehovah!” If you do, 1 Timothy 6:19 assures that you will ‘safely treasure up for yourself a fine foundation for the future,’ and you will “get a firm hold on the real life.” Obviously, we will be directing many of our comments directly to you dear young ones in Jehovah’s organization. Yet, if we think about it, all of us figuratively are very young ones compared to eternity —how long we hope to live. So, in a sense, our comments during this broadcast have application to all of us.
