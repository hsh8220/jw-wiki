## 도입부 (전사본 발췌) — ⚠️ 영어 자막 기반

Ephesians 4:30 has been selected as the theme text for my comments this month. Ephesians 4:30 says: “Also, do not be grieving God’s holy spirit, “with which you have been sealed for a day of releasing by ransom.” Well, what does it mean to “be grieving God’s holy spirit”? Before we discuss that, let’s make clear what grieving the spirit is not.
