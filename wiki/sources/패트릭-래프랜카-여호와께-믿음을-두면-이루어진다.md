## 도입부 (전사본 발췌) — ⚠️ 영어 자막 기반

During his earthly ministry, Jesus miraculously healed many sick or disabled persons. At times, he would say to such ones the expression we find at Matthew 9:29: “According to your faith let it happen to you.” The principle of what Jesus stated applies in every aspect of our Christian life and ministry. But today we will discuss how this principle that faith in Jehovah makes things happen can work for us in three areas.
