The text today is taken from The Watchtower of 2016, January, and the theme “Be Compelled by God’s ‘Indescribable Free Gift.’” Well, this Memorial season really does move us to contemplate what Jehovah has done for us, and we should be compelled. The Insight volume has a very interesting article on this matter of forgiveness. And in one of the statements it mentions that “the Hebrew verb sa·lachʹ “[for] (forgive) is used only with regard to [Jehovah’s] pardoning a sinner.” It’s never used in connection with a human forgiving a human.
